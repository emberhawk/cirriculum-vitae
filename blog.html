Introduction Post


I am writing a mini blog to give some explaination and understanding on why I created this site , it's purpose and how I intend to use it.

I was made redundant in August 2020 from my tech support role. Instead of squandering my free time under the coronavirus 🦠 restrictions I decided to learn new skills.

My career has not been going anywhere up to this point. I have had a varierty of short term jobs in the tech support sector. I have had a few roles including some volunteering but nothing of any major substance. So I decided to relearn a new skillset. I have always been interested in Video Game and I though I could try some game development to see if its a possibility. With the remaining redundancy pay I got myself a laptop and bought some Udemy Courses. I hear some reviews and there was no real possibility of going back to university or college at this point. Also at 36 I doubt that would not be a wise decision, at some point I need to make a living. Also there must be a job role there that could do with my skills.

The following posts will be talking about the courses I have been studying, what I have learned, what it will be usefull for, possibly some code examples and so on. The idea is to clarify a potential employers perspective or decision making into hiring me for a project.
